How do you know when you need a residential lease, sublease or license?

Lease

A residential lease agreement defines duties and responsibilities between a landlord and tenant

Sublease

A residential sublease usually spells out a living arrangement between a tenant and subtenant

License

A license provides a limited right to enter or use certain features of a property or its infrastructure

Before you rent your property to a tenant or subtenant, speak to a lawyer to reduce your liability. As a landlord or master tenant, help protect yourself from potential landlord-tenant issues by having a lawyer draft a residential lease agreement or sublease. As a landlord you want a lease to make sure there is a formal record of the agreement you have with your tenant. As a tenant you are responsible to the landlord so you definitely want a sublease with your subtenant just in case you need to protect yourself. The inverse is also true, if you are a tenant or subtenant and your landlord does not have a lease ready for you then give your landlord a lease form. Even if your landlord does have a lease form, have a lawyer review your lease before you sign anything. Whether you need a rental lease agreement drafted or as a prospective tenant want a lawyer to review your lease, it pays to formalize your agreement in writing. If you are about to enter or are currently in a landlord-tenant relationship without a written lease then you leave it up to the default law of your state or a judge to decide landlord-tenant issues. A residential lease or sublease makes it o cial and allows you to agree on lease terms such as the length of the lease, amount of rent or security deposit by putting it in writing. If you are a homeowner who wants to rent a room in your home to a tenant then you may need a room rental agreement. If you are looking to rent a driveway or garage in your house, a license agreement may be the best option. There are many di erent rental agreements to choose from. Even you are simply agreeing to split utilities between roommates and need a utilities agreement form, it makes sense to use a lawyer.
